Research Statement
"My BSc thesis investigates the cross-population generalizability of radiomics and dosiomics for predicting radiation pneumonitis in a South Asian cohort — a clinically underrepresented domain with direct relevance to equitable AI deployment in oncology."

I am an emerging medical physicist with a peer-reviewed research record spanning radiation oncology, radiomics, CT image quality optimization, and AI-integrated biomedical applications.

My abstract was accepted at IUPESM World Congress 2025 (Sydney, Australia) and ECMP 2024 (Munich, Germany). I was awarded Best Poster Presentation at ICMPROI 2025 (Dhaka) — competing against senior researchers from across Bangladesh.

My clinical training spans Diponegoro University (CT physics, image quality metrics), Rayos Contra Cancer (18-hour certified EBRT course), and RAD-AID radiation oncology workshops. I combine rigorous physics foundations with hands-on expertise in Python, MATLAB, and treatment planning workflows.

2025 – 2026 BSc Thesis Open Source · MIT
Cross-Population Generalizability of Lung Cancer Radiomics & Dosiomics: Implications for AI Deployment in a South Asian Cohort
Spearheaded a lung cancer analytical study utilizing clinical imaging data from Square Hospital and Ahsania Mission Cancer & General Hospital, Dhaka. Engineered computational models applying AI-driven bioinformatics to analyze domain shifts and predict radiation pneumonitis. Quantified cross-population generalizability metrics, addressing critical underrepresentation of South Asian cohorts in radiomics/dosiomics validation pipelines. Full codebase published under MIT License.

Supervisors: Hozzatul Islam Sayed · Dr. Md Anwarul Islam (Square Hospitals) · Dr. M. Jahangir Alam (AMCGH)
Python PyRadiomics PyTorch SHAP Jupyter DICOM 3D Slicer
2025 – Present In Progress
Post-Surgery Rehabilitation EMG Nerve Tracking Device
Initiated design and prototyping of a low-cost EMG device using Arduino Uno and surface electrodes for post-surgical muscle activation monitoring. Programmed initial data acquisition in C; conducted preliminary hardware testing and literature review on advanced signal filtering (MF, AMF, BF, NLM noise reduction techniques).
